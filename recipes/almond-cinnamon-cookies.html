Almond Butter Cinnamon Cookies

Description

This recipe is perfect if you're looking for a delicious low-carb snack. Below you will find the ingredients needed to make this delightful snack! If you're on a keto diet this is perfect for you!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups blanched almond flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup low-calorie natural sweetener (such as Swerve)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon sugar-free vailla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix almond flour, butter, sweetener, egg, vanilla, and cinnamon together in a bowl untill well combined. Roll dough into twelve even 1 inch balls. Place balls oto the prepared baking sheet. Press down twice with a fork onto each ball to make a crisscross.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven untill the edges are golden, 12 to 15 minutes. Cool on the baking sheet briefly before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely
