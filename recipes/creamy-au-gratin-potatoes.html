Creamy au Gratin Potatoes

Description

This dish is perfect for a side. This recipe will be everyone's favorite dish at a family dinner. This recipe will leave your family wanting more! Below you will learn how to make them and the required ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium russet potatoes, sliced thinly
  • 1 medium onion, sliced into rings
  • salt and ground black pepper for taste
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 ½ cups shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400℉. Butter a 1-quart casserole dish.
  2. Layer ½ of the potatoes in the bottom of the prepared casserole dish; season with salt and pepper to your liking. Layer onion slices over the top, then top with the remaining potatoes. Seasonm again to your liking.
  3. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Gradually whisk in flour and salt and cook, whisking constantly until raw flour flavor has cooked off, about 1 minute. Gradually add milk, about ¼ cup at a time, whisking well after each addition to incorporate; the gradual addition and whisking of milk will help avoid lumps in your sauce.
  4. Cook, whisking constantly, untill the mixture has thickened, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in cheese all at once; continue stirring untill melted, 30 to 60 seconds. Pour cheese sauce over the potatoes, and cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until potatoes are tender and sauce is bubbly, about 1 &189# hours.
